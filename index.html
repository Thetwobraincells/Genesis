About GENESIS

GENESIS is a 30-hour offline hackathon where brilliant minds come together to build innovative solutions for challenging problem statements. We aim to foster problem-solving and innovation among students in high-impact domains.

Participants will tackle real-world challenges in AI, FinTech, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Sustainability, and Healthcare. It's a non-stop marathon of coding, creativity, and collaboration!

About Quest-IT

Quest-IT, the VESIT Technical Cell, is a hub dedicated to fostering innovation and technological excellence. With 4 years of experience, our mission is to empower students with practical skills through engaging workshops, hackathons, and a collaborative community.

Frequently Asked Doodles

Who can participate?

GENESIS is open to all students of VESIT. You can be from any branch or year!

How much does it cost?

Absolutely nothing! Participation is 100% free for all selected students. Plus, there's free food, drinks, and swag!

What if I don't have a team?

No problem! You can register as an individual. We will have a team-matching session on Discord and at the event itself so you can find other awesome people to work with.

What should I bring?

Your laptop, chargers, any necessary hardware, a sleeping bag (if you plan to nap!), and a whole lot of enthusiasm.
